What we do

We express the fine art of living
through our food

Cuisine is a contemporary restaurant, and the culinary extension of our founder's concept to celebrate and elevate all aspects of lifestyle. At Cuisine, we reinvent classics of cooking, giving foods with which we are familiar an innovative twist.

Nestled within a lush bamboo-framed garden, Cuisine is an intimate dining space displaying contemporary Art and Design from the all over the world. With our experienced chefs, Cuisine aims to give you a rare dining experience that celebrates the distinctive flavours from all over the world.
